Architecture design principles are fundamental guidelines that help shape the structure and organization of a system, ensuring it meets both functional and non-functional requirements. These principles include modularity, which promotes dividing a system into distinct components that can be developed, tested, and maintained independently, thereby enhancing scalability and maintainability.
